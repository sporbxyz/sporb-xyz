About SPORB Protocol

The people building the orbital data layer for DePIN.

Mission

SPORB Protocol builds decentralized physical infrastructure (DePIN) that bridges real-world data with on-chain incentives. By connecting orbital sensor networks, edge hardware, and verifiable computation, we create a trustless pipeline where physical-world signals — from spectrum readings to environmental metrics — become programmable, rewardable assets on the Solana blockchain. Our mission is to make the data layer of the physical world as open, permissionless, and resilient as the internet itself.
