FAQ

What's the difference between Lite and Miner?

Lite is a smart display that mines $SPORB. Miner has a powerful RK3588 chip with 6 TOPS NPU — it contributes compute power to the network and earns 3-5x more rewards.

When will my Node ship?

Target Q1 2027. Pre-sale funds production. Monthly progress updates.

How do I earn $SPORB?

Keep the node powered on. It displays market data and sends heartbeats. Rewards from the daily Dynamic Pool.

Can I buy multiple Nodes?

Yes — no limit. Each node earns independently. Mix Lite and Miner for a balanced setup.

What about bonus $SPORB?

From Ecosystem allocation. Locked 3 months post-launch, linear unlock over 6 months.

Can Miner nodes earn extra rewards?

Yes — Miner nodes have 6 TOPS NPU compute power. Future network upgrades will allow Miners to resell idle compute power for additional $SPORB rewards. More details at launch.

Token supply?

100B total. 10% circulating at TGE. Node pool: 0.001% (1M initial). Most locked/vesting. Pool expandable via governance as network grows.
