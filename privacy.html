Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Email address (when subscribing to updates or contacting support)
  • Wallet address (when interacting with the protocol)
  • Communications and correspondence with our team

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, certain information is automatically collected:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location

Blockchain Data

SPORB Protocol is built on the Solana blockchain. Any transactions you sign and submit to the Solana network are public by design. Your wallet address, transaction amounts, and interaction history with our smart contracts are recorded on-chain and are not under our control.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and gather analytics.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality and security of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve performance and content.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences across sessions.

Your Choices: Most browsers allow you to control cookie settings via their preferences menu. You may block or delete cookies; however, doing so may affect certain features of our website. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To detect, prevent, and address security threats or fraud

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

SPORB Protocol integrates with several third-party services to provide its functionality. Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

Solana Blockchain

The SPORB Protocol smart contracts are deployed on the Solana blockchain. All on-chain interactions — including token swaps, staking, and transactions — are publicly visible on the Solana ledger. We do not control the Solana network or its data handling practices. Please review Solana's Privacy Policy for more information.

Jupiter Aggregator

SPORB Protocol uses Jupiter as its primary decentralized exchange aggregator for token swaps. When you execute a swap through our interface, your transaction is routed through Jupiter's infrastructure. Jupiter may collect certain data related to swap routing and execution. Please review Jupiter's Privacy Policy.
