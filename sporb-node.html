🛡️ Buy Pressure & Burn Mechanisms

🔁 5% Revenue Buy-Back

Auto Buy

5% of all Signal Engine subscription fees
→ buy $SPORB from market monthly
→ distributed as bonus rewards to nodes

🔒 Stake Lock-Up

Circulating Supply Lock

1,000 nodes × 500 avg stake = 500K $SPORB locked
10K nodes × 200 avg stake = 2M $SPORB locked
Each node sale = more tokens taken out of circulation

🔥 Natural Cap

Mining Ceiling

When reward per node < hardware electricity cost + opportunity cost → no new nodes added
Market finds equilibrium naturally. No arbitrary hard cap needed.

Summary: Dynamic pool model ensures no single cohort gets overpaid. First 1,000 nodes get the best deal (3-month payback at fair token price). Late joiners rely more on price appreciation than raw emissions. Buy-back + stake lock create sustainable token demand that grows with network size.
