Everything you need to know about the SPORB Protocol

SPORB Protocol is a next-generation decentralized finance (DeFi) platform built on the Solana blockchain. It combines an AI-driven signal engine — the XAU Signal Engine — with tokenized real-world assets (RWAs) backed by physical gold. The protocol bridges the gap between traditional commodity markets and on-chain finance, giving users exposure to gold price movements, yield-generating staking pools, and node-based network participation.

SPORB is designed for transparency, security, and community governance, with all smart contracts verified on Solscan. Whether you are a passive investor or an active DeFi participant, SPORB offers multiple ways to earn and interact.

$SPORB is the native ERC-20 utility and governance token of the SPORB Protocol. It powers the entire ecosystem:

  • Governance — $SPORB holders vote on protocol upgrades, fee structures, and treasury allocations.
  • Staking — Stake $SPORB to earn rewards backed by XAU Signal Engine yields.
  • Node Rewards — Node operators earn $SPORB for validating and relaying signals.
  • Fees & Access — Used for premium signal subscriptions and reduced trading fees.

Buying $SPORB is simple. Follow these steps:

  • 1. Get a wallet — Download MetaMask or WalletConnect-compatible wallet.
  • 2. Fund with ETH — Purchase ETH on a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken) and send it to your wallet, or use a fiat on-ramp like MoonPay.
  • 3. Connect to the dApp — Visit the official SPORB Protocol website and click "Launch App". Connect your wallet.
  • 4. Swap — Use the built-in swap interface to exchange ETH for $SPORB at the current market rate.
  • 5. Alternative: DEX — You can also trade $SPORB on Uniswap V3 using the contract address available on the official site.

⚠ Always verify the contract address from official sources to avoid scams.

The SPORB Node Presale is an early-access sale of limited-edition SPORB Nodes. Each node is an NFT license that entitles its holder to:

  • A share of daily XAU Signal rewards distributed in ETH and $SPORB.
  • Priority access to future protocol features and airdrops.
  • A percentage of network transaction fees proportional to node tier.
  • Node tiers: Sentinel, Validator, and Oracle — each with increasing reward multipliers.

The presale is conducted in phases with escalating pricing. Early participants receive the best rates and bonus allocations. All node sales are capped and on-chain transparent.

Staking on SPORB lets you earn passive yield by locking your $SPORB tokens in the protocol's staking contract. Here is how it works:

  • Deposit — Connect your wallet, navigate to the Staking dashboard, and choose your stake amount and lock period (flexible, 30-day, 90-day, or 180-day).
  • Earn — Rewards accrue in real-time from XAU Signal Engine yields, swap fees, and node revenue. Longer lock periods earn higher APY multipliers.
  • Compound or Claim — Reinvest rewards automatically (auto-compound) or claim them to your wallet at any time.
  • Unstake — After your lock period, withdraw your principal plus accrued rewards. Early unstaking may incur a small penalty.

Rewards are distributed in $SPORB with a portion also available in ETH for higher-tier stakers. All staking contracts are audited and publicly verifiable.

The XAU Signal Engine is SPORB's proprietary AI-powered market analysis system. It processes terabytes of on-chain and off-chain data to generate actionable trading signals for XAU (gold) price movements. Key features:

  • Multi-Model AI — Uses ensemble machine learning combining LSTM neural networks, gradient-boosted trees, and transformer-based models for high-accuracy predictions.
  • Real-Time Data — Ingests live gold spot prices, COMEX futures, DXY index, macro-economic indicators, and on-chain volume data.
  • Signal Types — Generates Long, Short, and Neutral signals with confidence scores and entry/exit zones.
  • On-Chain Distribution — Signals are published on-chain via oracles, making them immutable and verifiable by anyone.

The XAU Signal Engine powers the protocol's yield generation — a portion of profits from signal-driven strategies is redistributed to $SPORB stakers and node operators.

Safety and transparency are core pillars of the SPORB Protocol. Here's what we have done to ensure the highest level of security:

  • Contract Verified — All smart contracts are verified on Solscan, with full source code publicly available for anyone to inspect.
  • Audited — The codebase has undergone comprehensive audits by leading third-party firms. Audit reports are published on the official site.
  • Team Tokens Vested — Team and advisor allocations are locked in a vesting contract with a 6-month cliff followed by 12 months of linear release, preventing any early dumping.
  • Liquidity Locks — Initial liquidity pool tokens are locked for a minimum of 12 months via time-lock contracts.
  • Multi-Sig Treasury — The protocol treasury is controlled by a multi-signature wallet requiring approval from multiple trusted signers.
  • No Mint Functions — The $SPORB token contract includes no minting functions, ensuring the total supply is permanently fixed at 100,000,000.
