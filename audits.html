Security & Audits

SPORB Protocol is built on a foundation of transparency, rigorous review, and continuous improvement. Our security posture is our highest priority.

SPORB takes security seriously. Every line of smart-contract code, every economic parameter, and every governance mechanism undergoes thorough internal and external review before reaching mainnet. We believe that true decentralisation is impossible without trustless verifiability — and verifiability demands uncompromising security standards.

Bug Bounty Program

The SPORB Bug Bounty Program is designed to reward security researchers and ethical hackers who help us identify and responsibly disclose vulnerabilities in the protocol’s smart contracts, web interfaces, and supporting infrastructure.

Rewards will be scaled by severity — from 50 SPORB for low-risk informational findings up to 250 SPORB for critical vulnerabilities that could compromise user funds or core protocol logic.

Full terms, scope, and submission guidelines will be published alongside the program’s launch on Immunefi / HackenProof. Stay tuned.

Security Best Practices

🔐

Wallet Security

Always use a hardware wallet (Ledger / Trezor) for storing SPORB tokens. Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone.

✅

Contract Verification

Verify all SPORB smart contracts directly on Solscan. Only interact with verified, audited code.

⏳

Team Vesting

All team and advisor tokens are subject to multi-year vesting schedules with cliff periods, ensuring long-term alignment with the community.

🏛️

Multi-Sig Treasury

The protocol treasury is secured by a multi-signature wallet requiring approval from multiple independent signers for any outbound transaction.
