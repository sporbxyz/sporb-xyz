Emission Model

The SPORB network launched with an initial emission pool of 1,000,000 SPORB allocated for node mining rewards. During Year 1, each node earns approximately ~685 SPORB per day, distributed pro-rata based on total active nodes.

  • Initial Pool: 1,000,000 SPORB
  • Year 1 Daily Rate: ~685 SPORB per node
  • Decentralized Governance: Emissions are fully expandable via DAO governance — node operators vote on emission adjustments, pool top-ups, and future reward schedules.
  • Flexible Model: As the network grows, the DAO can increase the emission cap, adjust distribution rates, or introduce new incentive programs through community proposals.
  • Pro-Rata Distribution: Your share of daily emissions scales with the number of nodes you operate relative to the total active node count.
