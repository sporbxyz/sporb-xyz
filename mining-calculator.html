Emission Model

The SPORB network launched with an initial emission pool of 1,000,000 SPORB allocated for node mining rewards over 4 years. The pool is expandable via DAO governance.

During Year 1, the network distributes ~685 SPORB per day total across ALL active nodes, divided pro-rata based on each node's Score.

  • Reward Formula: Your Daily = DailyPool × (Your Score / Total Score)
  • Node Score: Tier × Uptime × Stake × Multiplier
  • Initial Pool: 1,000,000 SPORB (expandable via governance)
  • Year 1 Daily Rate: ~685 SPORB/day total
  • Lite Multiplier: 1.0x  |  Miner Multiplier: 3–5x
  • Tier Multipliers: T1 = 1.0x, T2 = 1.5x, T3 = 2.5x
  • Pro-Rata Distribution: Higher-tier and higher-multiplier nodes earn proportionally more based on their Score contribution.
  • Decentralized Governance: Pool emissions are fully expandable via DAO governance — node operators vote on emission adjustments, pool top-ups, and future reward schedules.
