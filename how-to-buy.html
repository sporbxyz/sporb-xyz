Download Phantom from phantom.app — available as browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) or mobile app (iOS, Android). Create a new wallet and securely save your seed phrase.

Buy SOL from a centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, OKX) and withdraw to your Phantom wallet address. You need SOL for transaction fees (~/usr/bin/bash.01 per swap) and as the base currency for the SPORB pool.
